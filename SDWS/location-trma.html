Two Rivers Martial Arts has six locations in central Iowa. Classes are taught at each of these locations by TRMA black belt instructors. Advanced students from all branches attend Brown/Black Belt Class Friday nights at 6:00 PM at the Two Rivers Hub, 2017 Southlawn Drive in Des Moines, Iowa near the corner of Fleur and Southlawn.

Brown/Black Belt Class is longer than a typical class and offers experienced students an opportunity to work together on advanced techniques. During this class students spend more time on free sparring and self-defense techniques than is possible during a typical class.

For costs and other details, click on the name of the branch that you are interested in to the left. For general information on Two Rivers Martial Arts, contact us at:
